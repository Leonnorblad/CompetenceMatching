Job Description:
As our ideal candidate, you will design, develop, and optimize ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) pipelines for extracting data from various sources and loading it into cloud-based data warehouses. Your expertise in managing complex data systems and ensuring seamless integration of multiple systems will be essential. You'll also collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business requirements and prioritize projects.

Key responsibilities:

* Design, develop, and optimize ETL pipelines using Python libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and SQLAlchemy.
* Manage cloud-based data warehousing solutions (Amazon Redshift or Google BigQuery).
* Collaborate with stakeholders to identify business requirements and prioritize projects.
* Perform data quality checks and ensure high-quality data processing.

Mandatory Requirements (Must-Have):

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Engineering, or a related field.
* 3+ years of experience as a data engineer designing ETL pipelines and managing cloud-based data warehousing solutions.
* Proficiency in Python programming languages and libraries like Pandas, NumPy, and SQLAlchemy.
* Strong knowledge of data modeling concepts and SQL databases (e.g., MySQL).
* Excellent analytical skills with the ability to work under pressure.

Preferred Requirements (Nice-to-Have):

* Master's degree in Computer Science or Data Engineering.
* Experience with cloud-based platforms such as AWS or Google Cloud.
* Strong understanding of big data processing frameworks like Apache Spark or Hadoop.
* Familiarity with data visualization tools and SQL databases.
