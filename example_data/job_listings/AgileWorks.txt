* Identifying and mitigating risks and issues
* Facilitating effective communication among team members

• Relevant certifications (e.g., PMP, PRINCE2) a plus

Preferred Requirements (Nice-to-Have):

• 7+ years of experience in project management roles within agile environments

• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
• Familiarity with data analytics tools and techniques
• Experience with cloud-based platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure)
