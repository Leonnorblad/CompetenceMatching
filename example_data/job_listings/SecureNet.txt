Subject: Senior Cybersecurity Specialist - SecureNet

Announcement:
SecureNet has published a new role: Senior Cybersecurity Specialist.

Role and Key Dates:
- Role Title: Senior Cybersecurity Specialist
- Duration of the role: 12 months (extendable)
- Deadline for application: February 28, 2025 (23:59 CEST)

Location:
We are based in Stockholm, Sweden. Remote work possibilities will be discussed during interviews.

Job Description:
The successful candidate will play a key role in enhancing SecureNet's network security posture and handling incident response protocols. The primary responsibilities include:
- Conducting regular vulnerability assessments and penetration testing to identify and address potential security threats
- Developing and implementing comprehensive cybersecurity policies and procedures to ensure alignment with industry best practices
- Collaborating with the IT department to implement technical solutions for security-related issues

Mandatory Requirements (Must-Have):
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Cybersecurity, or a related field
- 5+ years of experience in cybersecurity roles, preferably within a similar industry
- Strong knowledge of network protocols, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to work under pressure
- Proficiency in Linux and Windows operating systems

Preferred Requirements (Nice-to-Have):
- Master's degree in Cybersecurity or a related field
- CISSP, CEH, or similar cybersecurity certifications
- Experience with security information and event management (SIEM) systems
- Strong understanding of cloud security and compliance frameworks

Additional Information:
The start date for this role is April 1, 2025. The selected candidate will be required to work 40 hours per week with some flexibility to accommodate business needs.

Application Instructions:
To apply, please submit your CV and a motivation letter in PDF format (max 2 pages) to [careers@securenet.se](mailto:careers@securenet.se). Please use the subject line "Senior Cybersecurity Specialist Application."

Closing:
We encourage early applications. SecureNet is an equal opportunities employer committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion.
