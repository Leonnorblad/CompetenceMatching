Subject: Opportunity for a UX Designer at DesignHive

Announcement:
DesignHive has published a new role: Senior UX Researcher and Designer, to join our Product Experience team.

Role and Key Dates:
- Role Title: Senior UX Researcher and Designer
- Duration of the role: 12 months (possible extension)
- Deadline for application: 15th March 2025

Location:
We are based in New York City, but we also allow remote work options for our employees. You will be expected to collaborate with our teams across different time zones.

Job Description:
As a Senior UX Researcher and Designer at DesignHive, you will play a key role in shaping the user experience of our digital products. Your primary responsibilities will include:

- Conducting qualitative and quantitative research to gain deep insights into user needs and behaviors
- Collaborating with cross-functional teams (product, engineering, and design) to inform product development
- Creating high-fidelity prototypes that balance business goals with user-centered design principles
- Presenting research findings and design recommendations to stakeholders

Mandatory Requirements:
To be successful in this role, you must have:

- A Bachelor's degree in Design or a related field (Master's preferred)
- At least 5 years of experience in UX research and design for digital products
- Proven expertise in user-centered design methodologies and human-computer interaction principles
- Excellent presentation and communication skills

Preferred Requirements:
While not essential, the following skills will be considered an asset:

- Experience with designing and prototyping complex software applications
- Familiarity with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Knowledge of Design Systems and how to apply them in a production environment

Additional Information:
The start date for this position is 1st April 2025. We anticipate the utilization percentage to be around 80% over the course of the contract.

Application Instructions:
To apply, please submit your CV in PDF format, along with a cover letter that explains why you are interested in joining DesignHive and how your skills align with this role. You can find more information about our company culture and values on our website.

Closing:
We encourage early applications for this exciting opportunity to join our Product Experience team. If you have any questions or would like to discuss the position further, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at [careers@designhive.com](mailto:careers@designhive.com).
