Job Description:
We're seeking an experienced Marketing Specialist to manage and develop our social media presence across multiple platforms. The ideal candidate will create engaging content, analyze performance metrics, and collaborate with internal teams to ensure brand consistency.

As a Marketing Specialist at MarketSphere, you will:

* Create high-quality visual content (images, videos, graphics) for our social media channels
* Analyze social media performance using analytics tools (e.g., Hootsuite Insights)
* Collaborate with internal teams (Marketing, Content Creation, Influencer Engagement)

Mandatory Requirements:

* 3+ years of experience in digital marketing, specifically in social media management
* Proven track record of developing and executing successful social media campaigns
* Strong understanding of social media analytics tools (e.g., Hootsuite Insights)
* Excellent content creation skills (writing, design, video production)
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Preferred Requirements:

* 5+ years of experience in digital marketing, specifically in social media management
* Experience with social media advertising platforms (Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads)
* Familiarity with graphic design tools (Adobe Creative Cloud) and content creation software
* Strong understanding of influencer engagement strategies
